## Project Overview

### App Name
*What will your app be called?*

Rydr

### Brief Description
*In 2-3 sentences, what does your app do?*

Rydr is an app where new drivers who have just gotten their learner's permit can log time for their 50 hours requirement. You are able to start a timer within the app that will run while the phone is off, or users can log their own data that saves for future reference.

---

## Target Audience

### Who is this app for?
*Describe the people who will use your app. Consider age, interests, needs, etc.*

This app is mainly for 15-18 year olds who have just gotten their learner's permit.

### What problem does it solve?
*What need or pain point does your app address for these users?*

It solves a problem where people often forget when they went driving and can't put it on their log or solves the problem of having to write it down individually each time after driving.

## Core Features

### Main Feature List
*List the 3-7 most important things users can do in your app. Focus on what, not how.*

1. Start a timer within the app that runs while device is off and saves automatically.
2. Add your own data/time to your already existing hours.
3. Get driving tips within the app with video guides.

### Must-Have vs Nice-to-Have
*Which features are absolutely essential for your app to work (must-have), and which would be great additions but aren't critical (nice-to-have)?*

**Must-Have:**
- A button that starts a timer and saves to hold for future reference.
- A tab to add your own driving hours that adds to the hours data.

**Nice-to-Have:**
- Driving tips with video guides for users.
- Be able to connect with your driving school to share your log with them.
